By delegating your work-load to your workers, you earn an income by choosing a worker from which you will earn commission and who will receive a bonus.

Specifically, both workers have continued to work on the hard task for 90 seconds following their previous performance. Your task is to now pick a worker, call them Worker A who you wish to earn commission from. Namely, while the workers continue to receive $0.10 per correct answer they submit, you will earn a commission of $0.20 for every correct answer your chosen Worker A submits. You do not earn commission from the other worker. Worker A will also receive a non-zero expected bonus themselves. Among all the managers in DataProcess Ltd, one will be chosen at random and their chosen Worker A will receive a bonus of $1.00.
