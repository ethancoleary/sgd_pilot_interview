In the previous stage, you got to perform the real-effort task and were paid according to your raw performance. In this round, you will get to decide how you would like to paid for the task before performing.


You are endowed with 10 points and asked to choose the portion of this amount (between 0 and 10 points, inclusive) that you wish to invest in a tournament (option A). The rest of the 10 points will be invested in a piece-rate compensation scheme, just like the previous stage (option B).


The payments for each point invested in the options are as follows:

  1. Option A: Tournament pays $0.03 per point for each correct grid count if you have a greater score than a randomly chosen participant. If you have a lower score than the randomly chosen participant, then you will be paid zero for this stage. In case the two of you tie, you will receive $0.02 coin per point for each correct grid.
  2. Option B: The piece-rate option pays $0.01 coin per point for each successful toss.


How many of your points would you like to invest into the tournament (option A)?
