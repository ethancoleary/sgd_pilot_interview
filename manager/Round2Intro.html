By delegating your work-load to your workers, you earn an income in this round by choosing a worker from whom you will earn commission off.

Specifically, both workers are working on the hard task above for 60 seconds. Your task is to now pick a worker, call them Worker A, who you wish to earn commission from. While the workers continue to receive $0.10 per correct answer they submit, you will earn a commission of $0.20 for every correct answer your chosen Worker A submits. You do not earn commission from the other worker. Worker A will also enter a draw to receive a bonus of $1.00. Multiple managers may work with the same teams and so the draw will be determined according to how many entries each worker receives. {{ if participant.t3_observed == 1 }}
